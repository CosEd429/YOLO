The Niffler is a small, furry creature with a long snout and a coat of black, fluffy fur. They are attracted to shiny things, which made them wonderful for locating treasure, but this also means that they could wreak havoc if kept (or set loose) indoors. Nifflers in general are usually harmless. They are native to Britain, Ireland, and Scandinavian countries. They are also very popular as pets.
