Credence Barebone was a wizard who lived during the 20th century. He was adopted by Mary Lou Barebone, the leader of the New Salem Philanthropic Society, a No-Maj anti-witchcraft group.Credence was physically abused by Mary Lou and was forced to suppress his abilities. He was the host of an Obscurus, a dark parasitic force due to his hatred and inability to control his magic. Credence was killed by Gellert Grindelwald in 1927.
