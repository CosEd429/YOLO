Albus Dumbledore during Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald was the first time we saw him in the Fantastic Beasts series. He was the one who sent Newt Scamander to New York to release Frank the Thunderbird. He was also the one who sent Newt to Paris to find Credence Barebone. Albus Dumbledore is a very powerful wizard. He is the only wizard that has ever defeated Gellert Grindelwald.
