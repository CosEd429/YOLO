Luna was a witch, and the only child of Xenophilius Lovegood and Pandora Lovegood. She attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was sorted into Ravenclaw. She was very eccentric and strangely perceptive. She joined Dumbledore's Army and participated in battles during the Second Wizarding War. She fought at the Battle of the Department of Mysteries and Battle of Hogwarts, which she survived. Luna later became a Magizoologist.
